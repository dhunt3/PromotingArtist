The Music Box is a two-story concert venue, restaurant and private events destination located on the West Bank of the Flats in downtown Cleveland. Across our two stages—a downstairs Supper Club and the upstairs Concert Hall—we host well-known national touring musicians and local Northeast Ohio talent. Nearly every night of the week, you’ll be able to hear a diverse array of genres—to name a few, blues, jazz, soul, Americana, rock, punk, roots and country—emanating from our space.
